I'm a 4th-year physics masters student at the University of Oxford with a deep passion for superconducting quantum computing hardware, RF/microwave engineering, and experimental quantum-limited devices.

My interest lies in utilising the theory behind circuit quantum elecrodynamics (cQED) and condensed matter physics with nanofacbrication to create experimentally innovative outcomes. I focus on taking scalable appoaches quantum computing hardware design with practical links that can revolutionise industrial applications.
